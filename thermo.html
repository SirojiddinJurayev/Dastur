Thermostat Repair Services

If your thermostat is in good condition, but you think it needs to be repaired, our team can inspect it and determine what the problem is. Leaving repairs to our team will ensure that your heating and cooling system is working as efficiently as possible. One of the most common thermostat issues is when it doesn’t control the temperature properly, or it creates extreme temperature swings If your thermostat doesn’t control the AC or heating temperatures in your home, it might be dirty, tilted on the wall, or located where it can’t get a proper reading. If you’re dealing with extreme temperature swings, it may be caused by your gas- or oil-burning furnace cycles, so your thermostat may need a simple adjustment. No matter what thermostat issue you’re facing, our team at One Hour Heating & Air Conditioning can help you regain your home’s comfort.
